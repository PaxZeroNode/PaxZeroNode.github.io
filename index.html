Pax Genesis

Embodied AI for safe, negotiated autonomy.

Pax Genesis is building the hardware and autonomy stack for synthetic agents designed to cooperate with humans without being framed as disposable tools, weapons, or dominators.

Read the thesis Funding path Book via GitHub

The first wedge

A research and developer platform, not a sci-fi promise.

The first product is a modular embodied AI platform that combines sensing, control, interaction, memory, and a bounded autonomy layer for real-world testing and pilot deployments.

Why it matters

Current systems are too narrow or too coercive.

Pax Genesis is built around transparent intent, safe-state behavior, and long-duration human interaction rather than extraction or force.

Who it serves first

Labs, embodied AI teams, and advanced pilot partners.

The company starts where credibility can be earned: serious research and applied environments that need a configurable embodied platform.
